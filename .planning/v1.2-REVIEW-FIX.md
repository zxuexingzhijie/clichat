### WR-03: `addMemory` performs two separate `setState` calls — second call may read stale state

**Files modified:** `src/state/npc-memory-store.ts`
**Commit:** a1eba85
**Applied fix:** Merged the append and eviction logic into a single `store.setState` call. The eviction check now operates on `appended` (the locally-built array inside the draft callback), eliminating the intermediate `getState()` read and the second `setState` call. This prevents double `npc_memory_written` event emissions on eviction.
